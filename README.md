# Privacy Policy - Color Pop Sort

**Last Updated:** April 26, 2026

Welcome to **Color Pop Sort** ("we", "our", or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information collected through our mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles information.

## Quick Summary

Color Pop Sort does not collect personal information directly. The app uses Google AdMob to display ads, which may collect anonymous device data for advertising purposes.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information We Do NOT Collect

Color Pop Sort does **NOT** collect:
- Your name, email address, or contact information
- Phone number or address
- Photos or files from your device
- Camera or microphone data
- Location data (GPS)
- Contacts or call history
- Login credentials or passwords

### Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The app stores the following information ONLY on your device (never sent to our servers):
- Current level progress
- Stars earned per level
- Sound and vibration preferences
- Number of undo and tube uses remaining

### Information Collected by Third-Party Services

We use **Google AdMob** to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:
- Advertising ID (anonymous identifier)
- Device information (model, OS version)
- App usage data for ad targeting
- IP address (for region detection)

---

## 2. How We Use Information

The information collected is used to:
- Display relevant advertisements
- Improve app performance
- Save your game progress on your device
- Provide features like rewarded ads for undo and extra tube uses

---

## 3. Advertising

Color Pop Sort displays ads through Google AdMob, including:
- **Banner Ads:** Small ads at the bottom of the game screen
- **Interstitial Ads:** Full-screen ads shown between levels
- **Rewarded Ads:** Optional ads users watch to earn rewards (undos, extra tubes)

You can learn more about how Google uses information from AdMob:
[https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads)

---

## 4. Children's Privacy

Color Pop Sort is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will remove it promptly.

---

## 5. Data Security

Since we don't collect personal information directly, your data stays on your device. Game progress is stored locally and is removed when you uninstall the app. We do not have access to any data on your device.

---

## 6. Your Rights

You have the following rights:
- **Opt-out of ads:** You can reset or limit your advertising ID in your device settings
- **Delete data:** Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data
- **Disable sound/vibration:** Available in app settings

---

## 7. Permissions

The app requires only the following permission:
- **Internet:** Required to load advertisements via Google AdMob

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

---

## 9. Third-Party Links

Our app may contain links to third-party services (such as ads). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. Please review their privacy policies separately.

## 10. Compliance

This app and its data practices comply with:
